Approach

Design Thinking

To design the best product, I believe that technologies have to be associated with a great user experience. During innovation process, I’m using the design thinking methodology to understand what issues my customers are facing, defined the challenges, ideate and find concepts. At the end, I iterate back and force between prototyping and testing with end users to quickly find the best solution.

Skills

Multi Disciplines capabilities

As a mechanical engineer, I know how to design systemic solutions. My passion and curiosity help me to have multidisciplinary skills. Thanks to massive open online courses (MOOC), I’ve learned different programing languages and methodologies to develop my own web sites, desktop & mobile apps or IOT platform. I need to learn every day and question all the time my skills and the way I work to be better. I’m not an expert in all technologies I’ve learnt but my background helps me to prototype solutions and work great with software or electrical engineers, designers and business men.

Product Design

From the specification to production, I use design thinking and value analysis to define product functions answering to customer needs. My knowledge in mechanical, electronical and software engineering allow me to propose solutions and understand those of other team members.

IOT

I design my own IOT solutions from the hardware to the user interfaces and data management in the cloud. In addition of the technical layer, I propose new services with great user experience using IOT data and remote monitoring to add more value.

Project Managment

I know how to manage complex industrial and multidiscipline projects. As a product owner or classic project manager, I manage quality and cost to give customers the right product on time. I know how to transform business goal in technical specifications.

Customer Focus

When I design a product, I always take a step back to be sure we don’t forget our end users and customers. We don’t create products for us but for other people so for me, it’s important to be sure that what we design is what they need.
